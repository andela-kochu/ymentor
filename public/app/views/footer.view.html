ABOUT YA-MENTOR

Ya-Mentor is the free mentorship and networking platform for your YABA suburb of Lagos State, Nigeria. Residents, Workers, Students, etc within Yaba and environs get on the platform to either coach others OR learn from experts referred to as Mentors. Sit back and relax as Ya-Mentor makes your Yaba experience a unique one

Developed by Andela team for CcHub HyberLocal hackathon
